lasagna

description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, cheese, and herbs. It is baked until golden and bubbly, creating a comforting and delicious meal.

ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper

steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce by cooking ground beef with garlic and tomato sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake for about 30–40 minutes until golden.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
