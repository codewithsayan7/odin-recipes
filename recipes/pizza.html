Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with dough, tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Spread tomato sauce on dough.
  3. Add cheese and toppings.
  4. Bake for 15–20 minutes.
  5. Serve hot.
