Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are soft, fluffy breakfast cakes usually served with syrup or honey.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Mix flour, milk, eggs and sugar.
  2. Heat a pan with butter.
  3. Pour batter into the pan.
  4. Cook until golden brown.
  5. Serve with syrup.
